Celebrate your best moment and happiness with beautiful floral arrangements and bouquets that we pour our hearts into and make with care. Cloud Nine is a small family business located in Sacramento, CA. We have a variety of flowers for you to choose from. Different types of flowers have meaning, and we want to create bouquets that will deliver the right messages to the right one. The order can be booked at least one day in advance for small bouquets and two to three months for event decorating. If you do not know where to start, come in with your project or idea, think about the person you’re sending them to and why and our experienced florists will help you achieve a perfect gift and stunning floral decoration for your events. .

Sustainable

Unlike other flower shops, Cloud Nine partners with sustainable farms that minimize waste, recycle water, and protect workers’ well-being.

Language Of Flowers

In a world where seeing each other is limited, the act of sending and receiving gifts in the mail becomes even more powerful. We are so grateful to work with flowers and plants that brighten peoples’ days.
